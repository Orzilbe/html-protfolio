About Me

Hi, I'm Or! I'm a Product & System Implementation Manager with a background in Industrial Engineering and Management (B.Sc.) from Ben-Gurion University.

My professional journey has always been about bridging the gap between complex technical systems and the people who use them. Whether I'm optimizing financial workflows or leading large-scale implementations for the Ministry of Education, my goal is the same: to make technology feel intuitive, not intimidating.

Currently, I'm expanding my horizons with an M.A. in Digital Game Design and Development at Shenkar. I believe that the principles of game design-engagement, feedback loops, and intuitive UX-are the keys to creating the next generation of software and AI tools.

When I'm not architecting workflows on Monday.com or experimenting with AI prompts, you'll probably find me exploring new soundscapes for my projects or spending time with my family.
